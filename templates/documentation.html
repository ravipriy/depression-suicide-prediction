MENTAL INSHIGHT

ANALYSIS OF SOCIAL MEDIA TEXT FOR DEPRESSION, SUICIDE PREDICTION

Abstract

In today’s era marked by the pervasive influence of social media and the increasing prevalence of mental health issues, innovative approaches to early detection and intervention are essential. This project, titled ”Mental Insight,” aims to address these challenges by analyzing social media texts to predict depression and suicidal tendencies. Drawing on machine learning and deep learning techniques, the project focuses solely on text analysis to identify individuals at risk of depression and suicide. By leveraging user-generated content from social media platforms, the goal is to develop predictive models tailored to individuals exhibiting depressive behaviors. The project offers a user-friendly dashboard powered by Flask and MySQL, enabling users to monitor their mental health profiles and track improvements over time. Additionally, a generative model provides personalized guidance to users based on their data, fostering a supportive environment for mental health management. ”Mental Insight” seeks to contribute to improved mental health outcomes by offering proactive intervention strategies informed by social media data analysis. By prioritizing user privacy and ethical considerations, the project aims to empower individuals to take control of their mental well-being in the digital age.
